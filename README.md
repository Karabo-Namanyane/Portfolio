The project is a data analysis of South American Mc Donalds sales for the year 2022.
The following question required answers through data analysis to determine the productivity of the business:
  -Was there an improvement to sales in 2022 relative to 2021?
  -Which country had the most sales in 2022?
  -Were the customers satisfied with the service?
  -How was the overall perfomance of the company for the year?

The analysis was to be perfomed on the data provided on the inputs worksheet using Microsoft Excel.
To ensure easy flow through the different worksheets, the buttons were made links via the Excel link feature to ensure that when they are pressed one is directed to a different worksheet.
To find out if there was an improvement in sales from 2021 to 2022 a line chart of the annual sales for each of the years was created and the graphs compared. It reavealed that 2022 had more sales all throughout except in May.
This means that there was indeed an improvement in sales from 2021 to 2022.
To determine which country had the most sales in 2022, a column chart was created. The chart revealed that Argentina had the most sales in 2022 while Chile had the least sales. 
To ensure whether these values can be used to compare the productivity of the business, it would be wise to take into consideration the populations of the countries and present these sales as percentages of the population.
To figure out if the customers were satisfied with the service, a radar chart was created. The radar chart revealed that a lot of the customers were not satisfied with the service and speed but were relatively satisfied with the availability,quality and hygene.
To determine the overall performance of the company for the year, the three Key Perfomance Indicators were utaken into consideration. All three (Sales, Profit and # of customers) are ranging in the high 80s, showing very high and satisfactory business productivity.
